What is bites?

Don't know what to do with the stuff in your fridge? Think no more. Bites will provide you recipies using the ingredients in your fridge.




How it works

Step 1

Input the ingredients that you have in your fridge

Step 2

Choose a which type on food you want to eat on the tabs on the left and choose a recipe

Step 3

Enjoy your meal! :)
