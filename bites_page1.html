What is bites?

Don't know what to do with the stuff in your fridge? Think no more. Bites will provide you recipes using the ingredients in your fridge.

How it works

  • Input the ingredients that you have in your fridge
  • Choose which type of food you want to eat on the tabs on the left and choose a recipe
  • Enjoy your meal! :)
